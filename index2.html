Part 1: The Base (Walk around the outside)
1. The Boy & The Tiger
FIND

The statue of a boy fighting a lion (or tiger).

THE STORY

A teacher yelled "Poy Sala!" (Strike, Sala!) to this boy. He fought the tiger, won, and started the Hoysala dynasty.
